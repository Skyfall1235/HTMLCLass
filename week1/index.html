Favorite Game of all Time: Titanfall 2

Titanfall 2 is my favorite game of all time, it's not the game I have the most hours in, but beyond all, it shaped how I look at game design and what games can do to be fun for the user. Titanfall 2 has a very good story, involving a robotic character who has snappy and fun responses even when delivered in a monotone voice. The mechanics of titanfall 2 are enjoyable and easy to get used to, and the skill floor is low and the skill ceiling high. I believe it defined the movement shooter genre of games and gave me a great idea of what “good sci-fi” is when it comes to the players' experiences. Even if I have more hours in other games like space engineers or marvel at the technological details of Helldivers 2, Titanfall 2 holds a special place in my heart that I think will be very difficult to replace.
